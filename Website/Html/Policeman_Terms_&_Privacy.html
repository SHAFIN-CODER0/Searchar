Police Signup Terms & Conditions

Our site’s police/authority signup requirements are as follows:

📋 Required Documents and Conditions for Police/Authority Registration:

  • 🆔 National ID (NID/Birth Certificate): A valid copy of your National ID must be uploaded.
  • 📸 Photos: A passport-size photo of yourself and your NID photo must be provided.
  • 📱 Mobile Number: A valid, verified, and updated mobile number must be provided.
  • 📍 Address: Your correct residential address must be submitted.
  • 🎂 Date of Birth: You must provide your date of birth for verification.
  • 📧 Official Email Address: You must register with a valid official email (e.g., example@police.gov.bd).
  • 👮 Badge ID / Police ID: Your valid Badge ID or Police ID number must be provided.
  • 🏢 Designation & Station Name: Your official designation and station name must be accurately mentioned.
  • 📂 Official Letter / Appointment ID: You must upload an official appointment letter or authorization document (PDF).
  • ✅ Acceptance of Terms & Conditions: You must accept our Terms and Privacy Policy to complete registration.

⚠️ Important Instructions:

  • Any false information or forged documents will result in immediate cancellation of your registration.
  • Verification of your submitted details may take some time to ensure authenticity.
  • Only authorized law enforcement or emergency service personnel are eligible for registration.
  • Your account will be activated only after successful document verification and approval.

Please follow these terms to ensure a secure and authentic registration process.
