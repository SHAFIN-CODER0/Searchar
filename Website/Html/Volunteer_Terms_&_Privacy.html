Volunteer Signup Terms & Conditions

Our site’s volunteer signup requirements are as follows:

  • 🆔 National ID (NID/Birth Certificate): A valid copy of your National ID or Birth Certificate must be uploaded.
  • 📸 Photos: A passport-size photo of yourself and your NID photo must be provided.
  • 📱 Mobile Number: A valid, verified, and updated mobile number must be provided.
  • 📍 Address: Your correct address must be submitted (manual entry required).
  • 🎂 Date of Birth: You must provide your date of birth to verify eligibility (18+).
  • ✅ Acceptance of Terms & Conditions: You must accept our Terms and Privacy Policy to join as a volunteer.
  • 🚨 Geo-location Permission: Volunteers must agree to share their geo-location during missions.
  • 💼 Occupation & Availability: Your occupation, availability (Full-Time, Part-Time, On Call), and transport options must be accurately mentioned.
  • 📂 Approval Process: All documents will be verified before your volunteer account is approved.

Important Instructions:

  • Any false information or fake documents will result in cancellation of your volunteer registration.
  • Verification of your submitted details may take some time to ensure accuracy.
  • By joining, you agree to act responsibly and maintain a safe and respectful volunteer environment.
  • Follow these terms and help us create a secure and trusted volunteer network.
